Taco Rice Bowl

A delicious and hearty taco rice bowl made with seasoned ground beef, fluffy rice, fresh vegetables, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for a quick and satisfying meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn, drained
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Sour cream for topping
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and follow package instructions to season the beef.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked rice, black beans, corn, and diced tomatoes. Mix well.
  4. Layer the seasoned beef on top of the rice mixture.
  5. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the top and let it melt slightly.
  6. Top with avocado slices, sour cream, and fresh cilantro.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve warm!
