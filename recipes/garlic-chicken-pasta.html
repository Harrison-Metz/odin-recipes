Garlic Chicken Pasta

This garlic chicken pasta recipe is a quick and delicious meal perfect for any night of the week. Tender chicken breasts are cooked with fragrant garlic and tossed with your favorite pasta, olive oil, and Parmesan cheese for a simple yet flavorful dish. It's easy to make and sure to please the whole family!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup pasta (your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken breasts to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side.
  4. Remove chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss with the garlic oil. Add sliced chicken on top.
  6. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
